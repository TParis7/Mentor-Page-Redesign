Get started for free in 4 simple steps

P3 guides you through every step of mentor onboarding. Here's your journey.

Download & Register

Get the P3 app on iOS or Android. Sign up with Google or email — it takes just 2 minutes.

Build Your Profile

Add your bio, LinkedIn, education, industry experience, coaching strengths, and a 60-second intro video.

Get Approved

P3 manually reviews every application within 5–7 business days. Once approved, you're matched and listed.

Start Mentoring

Receive questions from matched mentees, respond with video guidance, and track their milestones.

Mentor Features

Built for mentors. Designed for impact.

Everything you need to guide, connect, and support the next generation — right from your phone.

Smart Matching

Personalized connections, powered by AI

Students personalize their profiles with education goals, career ambitions, and availability. Our AI matches them with you based on industry expertise and mentoring style.

  • Matched by industry, career goals & personal needs
  • Students choose you — every connection is intentional
  • View mentee profiles before accepting a connection
Video Guidance

Answer questions with short videos

Mentees submit text-based questions and you respond with authentic 90-second video answers. Your videos are automatically added to the Explore library for all students.

  • Asynchronous — respond on your own schedule
  • Videos uploaded to the public Explore feed
  • +100 discussion topics with conversation starters
Share & Connect

Grow your reach beyond the app

Share your mentor profile via text, email, WhatsApp, or social media. Your profile includes your bio, intro video, and a link from the National Mentor Portal.

  • One-tap sharing via AirDrop, Messages, Gmail, and more
  • Shareable link from mentors.pulseofp3.org
  • Track your followers, mentees, and video engagement
